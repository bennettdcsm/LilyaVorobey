About

Lilya Vorobey is an interdisciplinary artist and designer whose career includes curatorial, sculptural, mural, performance, video, and photographic installations as well as critical writing and public practices in communities.

Works range from interactive sculptures, drawings, paintings to murals, furniture, displays as well as design and fabrication of whatever for the automotive and tech industry.
